About the days

For the first time it is held at the Catholic University of Santa María, the VI IBEROAMERICAN DAYS OF COMPUTER HUMAN INTERACTION is held, an event that was previously held at the University of Cauca, in Popayan Colombia and the V Days that were at the Benemerita University of Puebla in Mexico

The Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) area has as one of its central problems the insertion of the technological element in real situations and above all improve the quality of life through the implementation of appropriate applications, relevant according to the needs of the user, The VI Iberoamerican Days will have the topics of:

Our Goal

This event is the continuation of the I, II, III, IV and V Organized Days and is expected to be a forum for discussion and learning of topics related to HCI at the Ibero-American level, on this occasion the topics will be about:

  • Augmented and Mixed Virtual Reality for Education
  • Collaborative learning
  • Gamification
  • Emotional Interfaces
  • Adaptive Instruction Systems
  • Accessibility
  • Usability
  • Use of video games in education
  • Artificial Intelligence in HCI
